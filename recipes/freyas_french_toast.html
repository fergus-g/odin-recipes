Freya's French Toast

Back

After a long night of feasting in the great halls of valhalla, you can wake up with a mighty hunger. Freya is alwasy on hand to whip up a plate of her famous French Toast. This sweet treat will have any warrior ready to get back to the party!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Maple Syrup
  • Brioche Bread
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Beat 3 eggs, milk and cinnamon together until combined
  2. Soak bread in custard mix and fry
  3. Fry bacon and add to stack of french toast
  4. Smother in maple syrup as desired
