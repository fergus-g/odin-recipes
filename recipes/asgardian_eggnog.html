Asgardian Eggnog

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Rum
  • Eggs
  • Nutmeg
  • Sugar
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Whisk egg yolk and sugar together.
  2. Add milk, cream, salt and nutmeg and heat over a medium-high heat
  3. Once heated, use this mixture to temper the egg mixture by spooning in small amounts and mixing
  4. Add everything back to the pot and continue to cook, while stirring constantly
  5. Add in heaps of rum and serve
