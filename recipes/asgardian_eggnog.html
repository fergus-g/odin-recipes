Asgardian Eggnog

Back

Nothing warms the heart of Odin more than this delicious recipe for Asgardian Eggnog. This smooth, sweet and firey treat is sure to warm you right down to the cockles of your heart. This would melt the hearts of even the Frost giants of Jotunnheim

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Rum
  • Eggs
  • Nutmeg
  • Sugar
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Whisk egg yolk and sugar together.
  2. Add milk, cream, salt and nutmeg and heat over a medium-high heat
  3. Once heated, use this mixture to temper the egg mixture by spooning in small amounts and mixing
  4. Add everything back to the pot and continue to cook, while stirring constantly
  5. Add in heaps of rum and serve
