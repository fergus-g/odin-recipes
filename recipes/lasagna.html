Lasagna

This delicious lasagna recipe is just how Odin used to make

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Flour
  • Mince
  • Tomatos
  • Pasata

Steps

  1. Fry off the mince until browned
  2. Make a bechamel sauce using the flour, butter and milk
  3. Add the tomatoes to the mince, cook until soft and season
  4. Add pasata to the mince
  5. Create the lasagna by adding a layer of mince, then noodle, then bechamel. Repeat until the dish is full
